How to Add Video Thumbnail (Screenshot from your video)
========================================================

OPTION 1: Use VLC Media Player (Recommended - Free & Easy)
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. Open your video (about-me.mp4) in VLC Media Player
2. Pause the video at the beginning showing your face
3. Go to Video menu â†’ Take Snapshot
4. VLC will save the screenshot to your Pictures folder
5. Rename the screenshot to "video-thumbnail.jpg"
6. Move it to the images/ folder
7. Done! The video will now show your face as the thumbnail

OPTION 2: Use QuickTime (Mac)
------------------------------
1. Open about-me.mp4 in QuickTime Player
2. Pause at the frame showing your face
3. Press Command + Shift + 4 (screenshot tool)
4. Take a screenshot of the video frame
5. Save as "video-thumbnail.jpg" in the images/ folder

OPTION 3: Use Photos/Preview app (Mac)
---------------------------------------
1. Import your video to Photos app
2. Find the frame with your face
3. Export that frame as an image
4. Save as "video-thumbnail.jpg" in the images/ folder

The HTML is already set to use "images/profile.jpg" as the thumbnail,
so you can either:
- Use your profile photo (already set up), or
- Take a screenshot from the video and replace profile.jpg

Current setup: The video uses your profile.jpg as the poster image,
which looks professional and consistent with your profile photo above!
