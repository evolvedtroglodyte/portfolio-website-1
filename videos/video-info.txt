# Video Folder Instructions

## Required Video

Add your 30-second introduction video to this folder:

**Filename:** about-me.mp4

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 30 seconds (exactly or close to it)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- **File Size:** Under 50MB for best loading performance
- **Orientation:** Landscape (horizontal)

## Video Content Suggestions

Your video should answer "Tell me about yourself" and include:

1. **Introduction** (5 seconds)
   - Your name
   - Your current role or status

2. **Professional Focus** (10 seconds)
   - What you're passionate about
   - Your area of expertise
   - Current goals

3. **Personal Touch** (10 seconds)
   - Hobbies or interests
   - What makes you unique
   - Your personality

4. **Closing** (5 seconds)
   - Call to action (e.g., "Let's connect!")
   - Enthusiasm about opportunities

## Recording Tips

### Equipment
- **Camera:** Smartphone camera (4K or 1080p) is perfect
- **Microphone:** Built-in is fine, but external mic is better
- **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light facing you

### Setup
- **Background:** Clean, professional (office, bookshelf, plain wall)
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders visible, eye level
- **Distance:** Arm's length away from camera
- **Eye Contact:** Look at the camera lens, not the screen

### Recording
- **Practice:** Do a few takes to get comfortable
- **Energy:** Smile, be enthusiastic, show personality
- **Pace:** Speak clearly and not too fast
- **Professional:** Dress professionally, as for an interview

## Editing

Use free tools like:
- **iMovie** (Mac)
- **Windows Video Editor** (Windows)
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Free, cross-platform)
- **Clipchamp** (Online)

Edit to:
- Trim to exactly 30 seconds
- Add your name as text (optional)
- Adjust brightness/contrast if needed
- Export as MP4

## Script Template

"Hi, I'm [Your Name]. I'm a [your role/passion] focused on [what you do]. 
I specialize in [your expertise] and I'm passionate about [your interests]. 

Outside of work, I love [hobby 1], [hobby 2], and [hobby 3]. 

I'm excited about [your goals/future plans]. Let's connect and create 
something amazing together!"

## No Video Yet?

The website will display a placeholder if the video file is not found. You can:
1. Add the video later
2. Start with a placeholder
3. Record on your phone today (it's easier than you think!)

## Need Help?

Recording yourself can feel awkward at first. Tips:
- Pretend you're talking to a friend
- Focus on being authentic, not perfect
- Your personality matters more than production quality
- Practice your script 2-3 times before recording
- Do 3-5 takes and pick the best one
