# Image Folder Instructions

## Required Images

Add the following images to this folder:

1. **profile.jpg** - Your professional headshot
   - Recommended size: 500x500px
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Should be a professional, high-quality photo

2. **gallery-1.jpg** - Gallery image 1
3. **gallery-2.jpg** - Gallery image 2
4. **gallery-3.jpg** - Gallery image 3
5. **gallery-4.jpg** - Gallery image 4
6. **gallery-5.jpg** - Gallery image 5
7. **gallery-6.jpg** - Gallery image 6
   - Recommended size: 800x800px or larger
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Can be professional photos, hobbies, travel, etc.

## Optional Images

8. **video-poster.jpg** - Thumbnail for your video
   - Same size as your video resolution (e.g., 1920x1080)
   - Shows first frame or a nice still from your video

## Tips

- Use high-quality images (not blurry or pixelated)
- Ensure images are well-lit and professional-looking
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 2MB per image)
- Use descriptive file names that match what's in index.html

## Where to Get Images

- LinkedIn profile photo
- Professional headshots
- Conference/event photos
- Hobby/activity photos
- Travel photos (especially national parks!)
- Team/collaboration photos
- Project screenshots (if applicable)

The website will display placeholder images until you add your own photos.
